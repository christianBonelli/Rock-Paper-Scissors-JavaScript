Tutorial

Rock Paper Scissors

Game Rules:

Choosing Cards: Each turn, you will receive three cards to choose from. Select the card you want to play by clicking on it.

Winning the Game: To win, you must choose a card that beats the computer's card. Remember that "Rock" beats "Scissors," "Scissors" beats "Paper," and "Paper" beats "Rock."

Scoring: Your score will increase each time you win a turn. Try to reach the desired score before the computer does!

How to Play:

Selecting Points: Before starting, choose how many points you want to reach to win the game.

Starting the Game: Click on "Start Game" to begin. You will be presented with cards to choose from.

Choose a Card: Click on the card you want to play for that turn.

Winning the Game: Keep playing until you reach the desired score or until the computer does so before you.

Good luck and have fun playing!
